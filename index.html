About us

Roll Outdoors offers bike rentals and guided tours in the city of Rovaniemi. Rovaniemi is a small city surrounded by amazing terrain, it is the capital of the Finlands Arctic Lapland and home of Santa Claus. While riding your bike here you have a chance to see reindeer roaming around the forests and if you are lucky even some northern lights might show up. The trails and the hill of Ounasvaara are our favorite playgrounds when talking about mountain biking. They are just a 5-minute ride away from our mid-city-located shop at Hostel Cafe Koti.
